# **CATEGORY 1 — Market, Customer & Performance Signals**

**Purpose of the Category**

Create a trustworthy, shared signal layer that reflects how buyers, customers, and segments actually behave.

**This category exists to answer:**

*“What signals do we trust when deciding where to focus?”*

### **Consolidated Workflows**

1. **Unified GTM Signal Ingestion & Normalization**

    Aggregate transcripts, engagement data, funnel metrics, lifecycle signals, and product usage into a normalized signal layer.

2. **Segment-Level Performance Intelligence**

    Surface conversion, engagement, and revenue performance by segment, persona, and motion.

3. **Customer Language & Topic Mapping**

    Identify recurring themes, terminology, and concerns across buyer and customer conversations.

4. **Lifecycle Signal Mapping**

    Map how customer priorities and risks change across pre-sale, onboarding, adoption, renewal, and expansion.
